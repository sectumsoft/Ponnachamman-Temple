Sri Ponnachammaan Sahayam

Ponnachamman Temple is the ancient family temple of Valiya Rckenchaath Family Which is located in Peruvemba village situated in Palakkad District.The main God of this temple is ponnachamman is the ancestor of the family who seems to protect the family form all the problems with blessings

Gods

Ponnachamman

Ponnachamman is the main god in the family temple who has been worshiped by the family members. He was a member of Rackenchath family who was believed to have acquired some supernatural powers. Many stories are told about Ponnachammaan's divine power. Ponnamman, who possessed many skills, especially magic, used them for the well-being and prosperity of our family. He himself cut off the right hand of Sri Ponnachamman, who had never done sorcery. Because it is impossible to cast evil spells without the right hand.Indicating this the idol of Ponnachamman also dont have the right hand.

Lokaparameswari

Any divine spirit needs a god or goddess to awaken its power. Ponnachamman's upasanamurthy was Sri Lokapameswari. This Lokaparameswari of Deivapura is the Goddess of ootukulangara temple.The original ootukulungara bagavathi temple is situated in peruvemba. this bagavathi is also very powerful.Long ago shaktheya puja was conducted for bagavathi every year.

Muthimuthassanmar

The Rakanchathe family originated from a couple from Mannattil family and Mankurissi. They have a place in our temple. Puja of Muthi Muthassans signifies devotion and respect to the famous couple of Mankurissi and Mannattil. Muthi Muthassan Puja is for the oldest couple in Manattil. So it is essential to participate in this puja performed by the family members of Manattil. The oldest person in the tharavad is responsible for maintaining the purity of the puja room.

Ittiyassamman

Our Deivapura also has the deity of Sri Ittiyassaman, a classmate and confidant of Sri Ponnachamman, who always accompanies Ponnachamman wherever he goes.Only ponnachamman and Ittiyassamman has their on idols in the temple .Like Ponnachamman the upasanamurthy of Ittiyassamman is also lokaparameswari devi.

Vezhuthammar

Generally, in all clans, Nagas were worshiped . Similarly, in Ponnachamman's Deivapura, Naga deities are also worshipped as Vezhuthammar. Pooja is performed with vellari choriyal, noor and paal(milk). Only Brahmins are allowed to perform the yearly puja-Kalampuja.Maninagam, Karinagam, Nagakanyaka, Chitrakutam, Nagas Apart from these, the Brahmarakshash is also housed. Padma Mitt, Palpayasa strike pooja are performed for Brahmarakshash.

Kandakarnan

Kandakarnan is the kshethrapalan who is taking care of the deities of the temple.NOt only the kshethraplana kandakarnan also takes care of the family members preventing any types of harms entering the tharavad.The fisrt pradhishta while we entering the rackenchath deivapura is kanadakarnan who is honoured as a barrier for all the bad things.

Masa Puja

After 1990, on the second day of every Malayalam month, Masa Puja is held at Deivapura. It belongs to each individual. Pujas are also performed as a special offering. Masa Puja was performed by Vembath Krishnan Namboothiri and now it is performed by Raman Namboothiri.

kalam Puja

Kalam Pooja, which lasts for three days, is performed on the three days immediately following the chanthabhishekam at the Ootukulangara Bhagavathy Temple. Kalam Pooja starts after ganapathihomam.the first 3 kalams will be of ponnachamman,lokaparameswari and Vezhuthammar respetively. The 4 and 5 kalams are performed according to the offering of the family members
